Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI bio generator work?

Our AI analyzes thousands of successful Instagram bios to understand patterns and styles that perform well. When you input your preferences, it generates unique combinations tailored to your needs while maintaining natural language flow.

Is my data stored when I generate bios?

We only store the bios you explicitly choose to save. All other generation data is processed in real-time and not stored permanently. Your privacy is important to us.

Can I use these bios for commercial purposes?

Absolutely! All bios generated by our tool are royalty-free and can be used for personal or commercial purposes without attribution.

How often is the bio database updated?

We update our bio templates and AI models monthly to reflect current trends and language patterns. This ensures you always get fresh, relevant suggestions.

Is there a limit to how many bios I can generate?

No! You can generate unlimited bios with our free plan. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.
